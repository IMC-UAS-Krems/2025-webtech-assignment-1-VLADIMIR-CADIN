From Orizont to IMC Krems

I grew up in a family that always supported learning and ambition. After finishing my studies at “Orizont” Lyceum, I dreamed of studying abroad — to see the world and challenge myself. Today, I’m proud to call myself a student at IMC Krems University, where I’m turning those dreams into reality.

Sports & Life

Basketball Player

Basketball has been a huge part of my life for years. I started playing professionally for Comrat Horses in Moldova’s National Division — a team that taught me discipline, teamwork, and leadership. Now I continue my journey in Austria with Krems Basketball, bringing the same energy, passion, and love for the game wherever I play.
